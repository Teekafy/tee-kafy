About

Hi, I'm Teekafy,
A student and early professional who got tired of productivity advice that didn't fit real life.

Most of what I found online was built for people with corporate jobs, assistants, and unlimited time. I needed something that actually worked for someone juggling deadlines, side hustles, and trying to build a career from scratch.

So I built Teekafy as the resource I wish I'd had. Everything here is practical, no-fluff, and tested in real life, not just theory. If you're trying to pass your exams, land your first job, or simply stop feeling overwhelmed every Sunday night, you're in the right place.

"Consistency beats talent when talent isn't consistent."

What you'll find here

Teekafy covers three core areas that actually move the needle for students and early professionals:

Productivity

Time-blocking, planning systems, focus methods, and habit stacks that work even on your most chaotic days.

Skill building

Practical strategies for learning faster, retaining more, and building the skills employers actually care about.

Career growth

CV tips, goal-setting frameworks, and career advice that cuts through the noise, especially if you're starting with no experience.

Free resources

Templates, planners, and downloadable tools, all free. Because the best advice should be accessible to everyone.

Why Pinterest?

Pinterest is where I save ideas that matter to me, so it felt like a natural home for Teekafy content. Every pin is designed to give you a real takeaway, not just a pretty graphic. If a piece of content doesn't answer "so what do I actually do?", it doesn't get published.
