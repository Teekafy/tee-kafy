Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: April 2025

This privacy policy explains how Teekafy ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you share when you visit this website. We keep things simple and honest here — no legal jargon walls.

In this policy

  1. What information we collect
  2. How we use your information
  3. Cookies and tracking
  4. Third-party services
  5. Affiliate links
  6. Your rights
  7. Contact us about privacy

1. What information we collect

We collect information in two ways — information you give us directly, and information collected automatically when you browse the site.

Information you give us directly:

  • Your name and email address if you fill in the contact form
  • Your email address if you sign up for a free download or mailing list
  • Any message content you voluntarily send through the contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • Your browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on the site, and links clicked
  • Approximate location (country or city level — not precise)
  • Referring website (e.g. which Pinterest pin brought you here)

We do not collect payment information. Any purchases made through affiliate links are processed entirely by the third-party retailer, not by Teekafy.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your messages and questions
  • Deliver free resources you've requested (like our productivity planner)
  • Send occasional emails if you've subscribed — you can unsubscribe anytime
  • Understand which content is most useful so we can improve it
  • Analyse site traffic to improve user experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Full stop.

3. Cookies and tracking

Teekafy uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to help the site function properly and to understand how visitors use it.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — needed for basic site functions (e.g. remembering your preferences)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand page views, traffic sources, and user behaviour (via Google Analytics)
  • Third-party cookies — set by embedded tools like Pinterest widgets or affiliate platforms

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality. Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies under Settings → Privacy.

4. Third-party services

Teekafy uses a small number of trusted third-party services to run the site. These may collect their own data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — for site traffic analysis. View Google's privacy policy
  • Pinterest — as a traffic source and content platform. View Pinterest's privacy policy
  • Email service provider — if you subscribe to our mailing list, your email is stored with our email platform (e.g. Mailchimp or ConvertKit)
  • Vercel — our website hosting provider

We choose services carefully and minimise data sharing wherever possible.

5. Affiliate links

Some links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click the link and make a purchase, Teekafy may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate links are always disclosed near the relevant content. We only recommend products and tools we genuinely use or believe in. Our editorial opinions are not influenced by affiliate arrangements.

Clicking an affiliate link may result in a tracking cookie being placed on your device by the retailer. This is standard practice for affiliate programs and is governed by the retailer's own privacy policy.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent to marketing emails at any time
  • Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the form below or at the email address listed in the footer. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Contact us about privacy

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, how your data is handled, or want to request data access or deletion, please reach out:

  • Via the contact form at the bottom of this page
  • By email at info@tee-kafy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
